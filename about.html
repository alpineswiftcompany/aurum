About our company.

Aurum Energy Inc. is an energy focused corporation headquartered in Los Santos, San Andreas, USA. Conceptualized in 2019 and incorporated in 2021, the company encompasses all areas of the oil and gas industry, including power generation, oil production, refinement and distribution.

The company has significant interests in renewable energy sources and is actively funding research and development in the area. Primarily, new technologies in the wind turbine field are being developed and implemented under contracts from the State of San Andreas.

Our Subsidiaries.



Aurum Energy Incorporated makes use of subsidiary companies for some of its activities.

One such primary subsidiary is Xero Gas which serves as the main distribution body for fuel. Operating a variety of gas stations across San Andreas and Liberty City, it provides fuel for multiple types of vehicles, including aircraft.

Our Values.



We believe that doing the right thing in a professional but caring, respectful and honest way promotes and delivers a transparent organisation that people can trust. We have nothing to hide and believe that the high levels of peer and family accountability we hold ourselves to will stand any scrutiny. Aurum's people are trustworthy, good to their word and reliable in their dealings with each other and third parties.



Safety is fundamental to everything we do. It is not a manual or checklist but is inherent in every thought and decision. Everyone should be able to go to bed securely after a safe day's work. Accountability for safety rests with all of us.



We care passionately about people, our assets and our responsibilities to everyone. Aurum people are optimistic and active people who work hard to achieve their goals. We believe that having the courage to work on our own initiative or stand up for what we know is right is fundamental to achieving success for the company and ourselves. We will not compromise our technical or engineering integrity but firmly believe that a ''can do'' attitude to our work will help us be more successful.



Aurum people are creative in the way they approach business. We firmly believe in the importance of facts over opinions and that the best solution is not always the most obvious. Taking the lead to achieve our goals is important but we recognise that success is only possible when people work together in a focused, collegiate and cooperative environment which carefully recognises and manages risk to achieve the optimal result for the business and ourselves.
